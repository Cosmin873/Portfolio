# Bankist App

It is a bank app that allows you to perform some elementary operations such as: login into account, transfer money to another account, request a loan and close the account. The operations are shown on the left side and they update every time you perform another operation, dates are actual, there is a sorting button and if you don't perform any operation in 10 minutes it will log you off.
